# Antilities

## What is this?

Antilities is a lightweight mod designed to be hard to find while bieng VERY configureable compared to other free alternatives. It is built for Fabric 1.21.1 and focuses on closeted combat cheats.

### Servers That Detected It!

### Anticheats which detect it!

## Features

### TriggerBot

* **Smart Delay:** Uses vanilla attack cooldowns with optional random tick offsets.
  - **Note:** Setting smart delay to 0 is extremely detectable so 1 is the safest minimum to prevent flagging.
* **Target Lock:** Stays glued to your current target for a set time to prevent "shuffling" between enemies.
* **Hit Flick:** Simulates a subtle vertical mouse flick when you land a hit to look more human on recordings.
* **Panic Swing:** Randomly swings when you get comboed or hit from just outside your reach to mimic a real player's reaction.
* **Fist Sword-Delay:** Forces hand/fist attacks to follow sword timing to prevent unnatural spamming.
* **Advanced Filters:** Toggles to target only players, only activate when holding weapons, or ignore babies.
* **Safety:** Pauses automatically while you are in menus, shielding, or if the enemy is blocking.
### Aim Assist

* **Dynamic Smoothing:** Customizable tracking speed (Exponential/Linear) to prevent robotic snapping.
* **FBM Variance:** Adds organic micro-movements to your aim to mimic natural hand tremors.
* **FOV Falloff:** Assist gets stronger the closer your crosshair is to the target.
* **Target Drift:** Adds a slow sine movement to your aim point so it never looks perfectly static.
* **Hitbox Edge Preference:** Blends your aim between the targets center and the closest edge for maximum reach.
* **Aim Delay:** Customizable reaction time (ms) before the assist starts tracking a new target.

### Stealth & Utility

* **Ghost Intercept:** Commands (`/-tbot`, `/-aimassist`) are invisible to the server and do not show up in chat or help menus.
* **Vulkan Support:** FOV circles and Target lines are fully compatible with VulkanMod rendering.
* **Stealth Toggles:** Option to disable action-bar notifications when turning modules on or off.
